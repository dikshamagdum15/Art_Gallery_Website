BANARAS GHAT

Artist
Anuradha Thakur
Size
30 X 24 inches
Medium
Acrylic on canvas
Delivery time
7-8 business days
Art Code
DTRR93
Description
This painting beautifully portrays the serene ghats of Varanasi along the sacred Ganges River. The warm hues of the setting or rising sun cast a golden glow over the historic architecture and the calm waters, creating a tranquil and spiritual atmosphere. Traditional boats float gently, reflecting the city's deep connection to its riverine culture. The presence of devotees and visitors along the steps enhances the sense of life and devotion, making this artwork a mesmerizing depiction of Varanasi’s timeless charm.
