LANDSCAPE

Artist
Basuki Dasgupta
Size
91.4 x 61 Centimeters
Medium
Oil on canvas
Delivery time
8-10 business days
Art Code
KAH67
Description
This painting captures an autumnal forest path bathed in warm, glowing light. The trees on either side are ablaze with vibrant red and orange leaves, creating a tunnel-like effect that draws the viewer's eye down the path towards a bright, luminous center. The impasto technique adds texture and depth to the foliage and the path, enhancing the sense of a crisp autumn day.
